I really miss it.. its waves..its smell..its tranquility and anger..

I remember.. the last time I was at the beach, it was at midnight.. and I was with him..

It was really cold, not very cloudy, but the moon wasn’t so bright either

We saw a crab in the sand, and for the longest time we thought it was alive, but it was dead

We also saw a seagull swimming in the water, we thought it was a person at first.. Haha, we were so stupid

There were really big rocks too i remember. I can still see their silhouette against the night sky, and the shadows they casted on the sand.

It was a cold night. But we had our hand in each other’s, and we smoked together.

Where did the smoke drift to I wonder..?
