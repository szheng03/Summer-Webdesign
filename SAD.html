Things To Cry About

By Summer Zheng

^ open in new tab

When you think about it

there are lots of things to cry about

But what does crying mean?

I know this web of tears covers my face and traps my breathing

But what do the droplets mean

What unknown sorrows do you glide across my face with? What attention are you seeking?

I feel you now

You are warm, wet, slightly salty to the taste

Where do you want to go?

Is it my hair, do you want to strand beads?

Is it my pillowcase, do you want to paint?

Is it my throat, do you want to explore its depth?

Are you lonely and lost too?
